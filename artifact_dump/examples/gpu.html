No Three.js, no CDN — the sandbox forbids them. This is the bare metal: hand-written GLSL shaders, hand-rolled 4×4 matrix math, vertex buffers, and a real-time raymarcher, all talking straight to your GPU from one HTML file.

01 / VERTEX PIPELINE

A living mesh — displaced & lit on the GPU

Real geometry generated in JS, streamed into a vertex buffer, then deformed by 3D simplex noise inside the vertex shader and Phong-shaded in the fragment shader. Drag to orbit.

02 / FRAGMENT PIPELINE

A raymarched world in a single shader

No geometry at all — two triangles fill the screen and every pixel marches a ray through a signed-distance field. Smooth-union of primitives, soft normals, fresnel and a palette, evaluated per-pixel on the GPU each frame. Drag to look around.

SDF raymarch · per-pixel–
