## SECTION 1: STICK CABLE SETUP

### Heading:
**STICK CABLE SETUP**

### Instructions (verbatim):
1. Connect ground clamp to negative terminal.
2. Connect electrode holder to positive terminal.
3. Leave wire feed power disconnected.

### Diagram Description:
Shows the rear/side connection panel of the Vulcan OmniPro 220 welder with three labeled callouts:

- **Label 1** — Points to the cable connecting to the **negative terminal** (ground clamp connection). The ground clamp itself (yellow/black clamp) is shown at the far left end of the cable.
- **Label 2** — Points to the **positive terminal** connection. A red-tipped electrode holder is shown at the far end of this cable.
- **Label 3** — Points to the **electrode holder cable** connection at the machine panel (the cable running to the red electrode holder).

**What this diagram answers:**
- Which terminal the ground clamp goes into for Stick welding: **negative terminal**
- Which terminal the electrode holder goes into for Stick welding: **positive terminal** (DCEP polarity)
- Wire feed power: **left disconnected** for Stick mode

---

## SECTION 2: MIG CABLE SETUP

### Heading:
**MIG CABLE SETUP**

### Instructions (verbatim):
1. Connect ground clamp to negative terminal.
2. Connect wire feed power to positive terminal.
3. Connect MIG gun to wire feed.
4. Connect wire feed control inside welder.

### Diagram Description:
Shows the welder connection panel with four labeled callouts and the associated cables/accessories:

- **Label 1** — Points to the **negative terminal** connection (ground clamp cable). Ground clamp (yellow) shown at far end.
- **Label 2** — Points to the **positive terminal** connection (wire feed power cable).
- **Label 3** — Points to the **MIG gun connection** at the wire feed unit (the MIG gun cable/euro connector).
- **Label 4** — Points to the **wire feed control connection** inside the welder (control cable).

A MIG gun with trigger is visible at the top of the diagram, connected via a flexible conduit/liner.

**What this diagram answers:**
- Ground clamp polarity for MIG: **negative terminal**
- Wire feed power polarity for MIG: **positive terminal** (DCEP)
- MIG gun connects to: **wire feed unit**
- Wire feed control: **connected inside welder**

---

## SECTION 2b: FLUX CABLE SETUP

### Heading:
**FLUX CABLE SETUP**

### Instructions (verbatim):
Follow MIG cable setup but connect ground clamp to positive terminal and wire feed power to negative terminal.

**What this answers:**
- Flux-core welding reverses polarity compared to MIG:
  - Ground clamp → **positive terminal**
  - Wire feed power → **negative terminal** (DCEN polarity)
- All other connections follow MIG cable setup steps 3 and 4.

*(No separate diagram; references the MIG diagram above.)*

## SECTION 3: TIG CABLE SETUP

### Heading:
**TIG CABLE SETUP**

### Instructions (verbatim):
1. Connect ground clamp to positive terminal.
2. Connect TIG torch to negative terminal.
3. Connect gas line to gas regulator.
4. Connect foot pedal inside welder.
5. Leave wire feed power disconnected.

### Diagram Description:
Shows the welder connection panel with five labeled callouts:

- **Label 1** — Points to the **positive terminal** connection (ground clamp cable). Ground clamp at far end of cable.
- **Label 2** — Points to the **negative terminal** connection (TIG torch power cable).
- **Label 3** — Points to the **gas line connection** going to the gas regulator on the machine.
- **Label 4** — Points to the **foot pedal control connection** inside the welder (control/amphenol connector).
- **Label 5** — Points to a separate cable/connector (foot pedal or additional control lead at the machine panel side).

A TIG torch is visible with a purple/lavender tungsten tip shown at the lower left. A foot pedal control is also shown connected.

**What this diagram answers:**
- Ground clamp polarity for TIG: **positive terminal** (torch on negative = DCEN)
- TIG torch polarity: **negative terminal** (DCEN — standard for TIG on steel/stainless)
- Gas line connects to: **gas regulator**
- Foot pedal: **connected inside welder**
- Wire feed power: **left disconnected** for TIG mode
